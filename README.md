# Power-BI-Car-Sales-Analysis
This project shows the sales and distribution of various car brands and colours across different groups and years

## Features
- Car sales over the months
- Car sals over the years
- Preference of car colours
- Brand prices
- Dealer spendings
- Most sold company brands

## Tools used
- Power BI
